# AUTO COMM Ceiling Capability Blueprint

AUTO COMM is designed to become the autonomous commercial operating system for a $100M+ epoxy, polished concrete, decorative concrete, manufacturing, distribution, installation, and training enterprise with 70+ locations and 100+ employees.

## Full-Scale Capability Stack

1. WhatsApp-first inbound and outbound revenue engine.
2. Multichannel fallback through SMS, email, voice, forms, and web chat.
3. Multi-brand routing across Xtreme Polishing Systems, XPS Xpress, National Concrete Polishing, Polished Concrete University, and National Epoxy Pros.
4. National location routing and territory intelligence.
5. Digital bidding intake and estimator packet generation.
6. Contractor recruitment and partner network development.
7. Training enrollment automation.
8. Product, equipment, supply, coupon, and promotion automation.
9. RAG-grounded company and industry intelligence.
10. AI voice and missed-call recovery.
11. Executive command center and revenue attribution.
12. Vercel agents for validation, QA, lead scoring, compliance, sync, and campaign optimization.
13. Supabase operational ledger for contacts, consent, campaigns, conversations, quotes, locations, and audit events.
14. n8n workflow automation for orchestration.
15. Base44 super agent as apex coordinator.
16. AUTO_BUILDER MCP as governed provisioning and build-control system.

## Guarded Autonomy Model

The system should be maximally autonomous in analysis, planning, routing, validation, and reporting. It remains governed for outbound campaign launch, production database migrations, live deployment, pricing commitments, legal/safety/compliance claims, high-value bids, and escalated customer messages.
