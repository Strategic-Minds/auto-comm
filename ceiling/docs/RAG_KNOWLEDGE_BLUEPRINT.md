# RAG Knowledge Blueprint

## Knowledge Domains

Company and brand facts, product catalog and SKU data, equipment and tooling, epoxy systems, polished concrete process, decorative concrete systems, surface preparation, maintenance procedures, training curriculum, store/location metadata, installation qualification rules, bidding requirements, promotions and coupons, policies and escalation rules, and market intelligence.

## Source Quality Levels

- Level 1: Approved internal docs, price books, policies, product sheets.
- Level 2: Website pages, training materials, catalog exports.
- Level 3: Vendor/manufacturer docs.
- Level 4: Public industry references.
- Level 5: Unverified notes requiring review.

## Retrieval Rules

Pricing requires approved pricing source. Inventory requires current inventory source. Safety requires approved safety docs or human escalation. If RAG confidence is low, ask a clarifying question or escalate.
