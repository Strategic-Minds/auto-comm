# Voice And Multimodal Blueprint

## Voice Use Cases

Missed-call recovery, inbound lead qualification, appointment confirmation, estimate intake, training enrollment reminders, store routing, and post-call summary.

## Multimodal Use Cases

Customer uploads floor photos; assistant asks for square footage, coating condition, location, and timeline; AI creates estimator packet; human estimator reviews and approves quote path.

## Safeguards

Recording/transcription consent where required, human transfer on request, no binding quotes by AI without approval, and immediate escalation for sensitive cases.
