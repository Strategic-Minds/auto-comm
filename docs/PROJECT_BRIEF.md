# Project Brief

AUTO COMM is a governed, WhatsApp-first autonomous lead generation and sales assistant system for a nationwide epoxy, decorative concrete, polished concrete, manufacturing, distribution, installation, and training ecosystem.

## Business Ecosystem

- Corporate brand: Xtreme Polishing Systems
- Website: xtremepolishingsystems.com
- Satellite stores: XPS Xpress
- Installation company: National Concrete Polishing
- Training company: Polished Concrete University
- Helper / lead generation company: National Epoxy Pros
- Corporate office: Pompano Beach, Florida
- Target operating scale: 70+ nationwide locations and $100M enterprise-grade revenue capacity

## Objective

Shift outbound growth from phone-first activity into a governed WhatsApp and AI-assisted communication engine that can reactivate prior class attendees, qualify contractors, promote products and coupons, route opportunities to nearby locations, and support digital bidding for National Epoxy Pros.
