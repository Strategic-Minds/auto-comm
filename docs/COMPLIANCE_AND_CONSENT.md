# Compliance And Consent

## Core Rule

Do not treat a phone number as WhatsApp marketing permission by default. Prior class attendance is a warm relationship, but outbound WhatsApp marketing still needs an auditable consent position.

## Contact States

- `confirmed_whatsapp_opt_in`
- `prior_relationship_needs_repermission`
- `email_only_permission`
- `sms_permission`
- `unknown_consent`
- `do_not_contact`

## Safety Behaviors

- Honor `STOP`, `UNSUBSCRIBE`, `REMOVE`, `NO`, and equivalent requests.
- Suppress contacts globally after opt-out.
- Keep a timestamped consent event ledger.
- Use approved WhatsApp templates outside the customer service window.
- Do not make guaranteed claims about income, performance, cures, legal compliance, or results.
- Escalate pricing disputes, angry replies, legal threats, account issues, and high-value bids to humans.

## Launch Rule

Start with a 50-contact pilot, then expand only if template quality remains healthy, opt-out rate is acceptable, reply quality is positive, no unresolved compliance complaints exist, and human handoff SLA is being met.
